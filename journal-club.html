How It Works

Simple format. Open door.

01

A paper drops

One AI-in-medicine paper is shared ~1 week before each meeting, with a short intro and discussion questions.

02

We meet for one hour

A rotating facilitator gives 10 min of context. The rest is open discussion — clinicians, technologists, learners, all in.

03

Notes go in the archive

Summary, slide deck, and threaded discussion are saved here so anyone who missed it can catch up.
