How It Works

Simple format. Open door.

01

A paper drops

One AI-in-medicine paper is shared roughly a week before each meeting, along with a brief context note.

02

Sign up + pre-question

When you RSVP, you'll get one pre-question to think about while reading. It primes the discussion and tells us where the room stands going in.

03

Short presentation + discussion

A rotating member gives a short presentation on the paper (10-15 min). The rest of the hour is open discussion, with clinicians, technologists, and learners all in.

04

Notes go in the archive

Summary, slide deck, and key takeaways are saved here so anyone who missed it can catch up.
