Get Involved

Three ways in.

Pick the path that fits — whether you want to join the community, propose research, or bring a product to validate.

Join the lab → Submit a project idea → Industry partners →

01 · Join

Join the lab.

For anyone at USF — student, resident, faculty, staff — who wants to be part of the TRAINABULL community. You'll get journal club invites, project announcements, and a seat at the table.

  • ✓Monthly journal club invitations
  • ✓Updates on new projects looking for collaborators
  • ✓Access to shared resources and curricula
  • ✓A directory of cross-disciplinary collaborators across USF

Form coming soon. Until the join form is live, email Karim directly with a short note on your background and what brings you to clinical AI.

Email to join

02 · Ideas

Submit a project idea.

Got a question, a half-baked pilot, or a problem you keep running into in clinic? Share it. We'll workshop it with the community, find collaborators, and figure out the right next step.

  • ✓Clinical AI research questions
  • ✓Quality-improvement or workflow problems worth studying
  • ✓Curriculum or training ideas
  • ✓Tools or pilots you'd like help validating

Two ways to submit:

Email an idea Open a GitHub issue

03 · Industry

For AI companies & startups.

If you build clinical AI and want real-world feedback from clinicians, researchers, and educators — start here. We're particularly interested in tools that touch primary care, medical education, and assessment.

  • ✓Product evaluation with practicing clinicians
  • ✓Pilot studies in real clinical and educational settings
  • ✓Co-authored research and peer-reviewed publications
  • ✓Curriculum collaborations and educational content

Tell us about your product. Include a brief description, the stage you're at, and what kind of validation or collaboration you're looking for.
