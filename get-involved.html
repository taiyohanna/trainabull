Get Involved

Four ways in.

Pick the path that fits. Each opens a pre-structured email so your submission lands with everything we need to act on it.

Join the lab → Submit a project idea → Industry partners → Get announcements →

01 · Join

Join the lab.

For anyone at USF (student, resident, faculty, staff) who wants to be part of the TRAIN-A-BULL community. You'll get journal club invites, project announcements, and a seat at the table.

Your email should include

  • Name + USF affiliation (school / department / program)
  • Role (student, resident/fellow, faculty, staff)
  • What brings you here (2-3 sentences on your background and interest in clinical AI)
  • What you'd like to be part of (journal club, projects, curriculum, industry work, open to anything)
  • What you bring (optional - skills, prior work, ideas)

Opens your default email client with a structured template addressed to khanna@usf.edu.

02 · Ideas

Submit a project idea.

Got a question, a half-baked pilot, or a problem you keep running into in clinic? We use a PICO frame to keep submissions sharp and reviewable - it works for clinical, educational, and informatics projects alike.

PICO format

P
Population / setting (e.g., adult primary-care patients with diabetes; PGY-1 residents at TGH)
I
Intervention / AI tool or approach (a model, a workflow, a curriculum, an evaluation)
C
Comparison / current state (what's happening now without it?)
O
Outcome (what does success look like? measurable + time horizon)

Plus: stage (hunch / concept / pilot / funded), help needed (clinical, technical, informatics, education, stats, industry), and your name + USF affiliation.

03 · Industry

For AI companies & startups.

If you build clinical AI and want real-world feedback from clinicians, researchers, and educators, start here. We're particularly interested in tools that touch primary care, medical education, and assessment.

Your email should include

  • Company + one-sentence product description
  • Stage (pre-product / beta / commercial)
  • Healthcare domain (primary care, ambient documentation, decision support, medical education, imaging, assessment...)
  • What you're looking for (clinical evaluation, pilot deployment, co-authored research, curriculum integration, advisory input)
  • Prior validation / current pilot sites
  • Contact (name, role, email)
  • Timelines, key questions, constraints

04 · Stay in the loop

Get announcements.

Not ready to join, propose a project, or partner - but want to know what we're up to? Drop your email and we'll add you to the announcement list (journal club papers, new projects, events).

Submitting opens your email client with a pre-filled subscribe request to khanna@usf.edu. No spam, no third parties - just announcements.
