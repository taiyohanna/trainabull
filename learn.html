Running essays from Karim on AI in medical education, primary care, and residency training. Practical, fast, and rooted in the clinic.

Fellowship

USF/Moffitt Clinical Informatics Fellowship

A two-year ACGME-accredited fellowship sponsored by USF Morsani's Department of Internal Medicine and based at Moffitt Cancer Center. Trains board-eligible physicians in clinical informatics across both Cerner (Moffitt) and Epic (USF/TGH) environments.

Visit Fellowship

Research Support

USF Health RISE Office

Research, Innovation & Scholarly Endeavors. USF Morsani's office that supports students, residents, fellows, and faculty mentors with study design, statistical consultation, scientific writing, and conference / publication pathways.
